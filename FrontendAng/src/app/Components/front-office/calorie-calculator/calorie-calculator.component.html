About Our
TDEE Calculator

This TDEE Calculator provides an estimated number of daily calories you need to reach your fitness goals. By entering your age, weight, height, activity level, and goal (lose weight, gain weight, or maintain weight), the calculator uses the Harris-Benedict equation to determine your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and adjusts it based on your activity level and goal.
